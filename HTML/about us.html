Insignia is one of the fastest growing online fashion stores in Sri Lanka. Offering a wide variety of clothes and accessories to fit any occasion from a house party to the most professional business function. Our staples are weathered globally and ethically manufactured in our own factories. We offer our collections to Men, Women, Kids. Our goal is to bring sleek and uniqueness to our customer no matter the occasion.


Insignia has been growing since it was found in 1899 in a small store in Colombo by Mr.Rajapathirane. By his perserverance and dedication Insignia has become one of the fastest growing retail chains and currently, the number one online fashion store in Sri Lanka.
